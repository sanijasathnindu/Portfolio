About

I am a passionate and detail-oriented front-end developer focused on building modern, responsive, and highly interactive web applications. My main role revolves around HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and UI development using frameworks like React and Angular, combined with strong design understanding from Figma.

I enjoy turning ideas and designs into fully functional, real-world web experiences. Whether it’s a landing page, dashboard, or full web application, I focus on creating clean, structured, and scalable front-end systems that perform smoothly across all devices and screen sizes.

With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I pay close attention to layout, spacing, typography, and responsiveness. I also work with modern frameworks like React and Angular to build dynamic, component-based applications that are efficient, maintainable, and user-friendly.

Alongside front-end development, I have experience in C# for building software applications and working with backend logic. This helps me understand how full systems work beyond the UI, improving my ability to design better structured and more practical web solutions.

I also use Figma for UI/UX design understanding and workflow planning, which helps me bridge the gap between design and development. This allows me to create interfaces that are visually consistent, intuitive, and aligned with modern design standards.

I enjoy experimenting with interactive features, animations, and dashboard-style systems where logic and UI come together. My approach is always focused on clean code, reusable components, and performance optimization.

I am continuously improving my skills by building real projects, exploring new front-end technologies, and refining both my development and design sense. My goal is to grow as a strong front-end developer capable of delivering professional-grade web applications while also expanding my knowledge in software development with C#.
